Music

Music has always been my passion, starting when I picked up the guitar at age 10, and later I taught myself bass, drums, how to write lyrics, and how to mix it all together.


The McJoes

My first band, The McJoes, was where I played electric guitar and learned to blend it with other instruments to create original tracks. Our lineup included Arthur Morgan on bass and James Morgan on drums. Although we never recorded professionally, we later released an album of tracks recorded on our phones.

   

Thomas Zaffalon

I decided to pursue my own path, experimenting with different styles of music. I’ve created instrumental albums, attempted singing (which didn’t go too well), and I’m still exploring new ideas, often collaborating with friends.

   

The Fallen Hot-Dogs

I formed my own band in 2023 with drummer Nicolò Valle, and it's been more amazing than I ever hoped. We began producing instrumental tracks, and our first album was released in 2024, showcasing the incredible voice of our new vocalist, Martina Agueci.

   

Spiked Flower

Spiked Flower is my most recent musical project, an instrumental duo formed with Arthur Morgan, previously a member of The McJoes. The project was conceived as a two-piece band with the goal of developing an original sound through the release of multiple EPs.
